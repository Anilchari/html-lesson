AEM Manager


An AEM Manager, or Adobe Experience Manager Manager, is a managerial role in the IT industry. According to recent trends, there is expected to be an increase in the number of job openings for AEM Manager in 2022 with over 9000+ openings. The average salary for an AEM Manager is 4.85L.

In terms of job perks, many companies offer attractive benefits packages that may include health insurance, retirement plans, flexible work hours, and bonuses. Additionally, some companies offer unique perks like on-site gym facilities, free food, or company events.

The scope of an AEM Manager includes overseeing the development and implementation of Adobe Experience Manager solutions for web applications. They work with clients to understand their needs, manage the project lifecycle, and ensure that project deliverables are met on time and within budget. AEM Managers must have a deep understanding of Adobe Experience Manager, as well as project management skills.
